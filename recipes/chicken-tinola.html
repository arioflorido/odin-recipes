Chicken Tinola

Description

Delicious chicken soup originated from the Philippines.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilo of chicken
  • 1/2 cup of sliced raw papaya or chayote
  • 1/2 cup of sliced ginger
  • 1 cup of hot pepper or moringga leaves
  • 1/8 cup of garlic
  • 1/8 cup of onion
  • 6 cups of water
  • 1 Knorr chicken cube

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into small serving pieces.
  2. Sauté the garlic, onion, and ginger for a few minutes - just until the onion gets soft.
  3. Add the chicken to the pan - sauté until the outer part turns light brown.
  4. Add water and simmer until it starts to boil.
  5. Remove scums and oil floating on the soup.
  6. Add 1 chicken cube
  7. Add hot pepper or moringga leaves
  8. Add sliced papaya or chayote
  9. Optional: Add fish sauce to to taste
